About Us

Legado Cycling is a Salt Lake City–based competitive cycling team with Latin roots, focused on building a strong local racing community. We raced actively throughout the 2025 season, showing up consistently across criteriums, road races, and endurance events in Utah and the Mountain West.

Who we are

Legado Cycling was built on a simple idea: show up, ride hard, and enjoy the process. We may be rough around the edges, but we are fully committed—to the sport, to each other, and to the relentless pursuit of improvement. Our team carries a strong Latin spirit: passionate, resilient, and never afraid of a challenge. We ride with intensity, a bit of sarcasm, and a lot of heart. Whether in training or on race day, our goal is the same—to push ourselves, represent our community, and leave everything on the road.
