About Us

🚧 This website is still under construction. Some sections are incomplete and links may change as we continue building the team’s online home.

Legado Cycling is a Salt Lake City–based competitive cycling team with Latin roots, focused on building a strong local racing community. We raced actively throughout the 2025 season, showing up consistently across criteriums, road races, and endurance events in Utah and the Mountain West.

Mission

We may not be the biggest team, but we will always be there fighting for the race — riding aggressively, supporting each other, and representing our community.
